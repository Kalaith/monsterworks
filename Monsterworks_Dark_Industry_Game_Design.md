## üéØ Game Overview

### Core Concept
**Monsterworks: Dark Industry** is a factory automation game where traditional machinery is replaced by living monsters who serve as workers, processors, and infrastructure. Players guide hunted monsters in building their own industrial civilization, turning the classic "heroes farm monsters" narrative on its head.

### Genre & Inspirations
- **Primary Genre:** Factory Automation / Logistics Optimization
- **Core Inspirations:**
  - **Factorio/Satisfactory:** Production chains, optimization, scaling complexity
  - **The Settlers:** Transport logistics, "living" feel of creatures carrying goods
  - **Dungeon Keeper/Overlord:** Villainous theme making the "evil" side sympathetic

### Core Fantasy
You are the collective will of hunted monsters, guiding them to reclaim their dignity and carve out a new homeland. Where adventurers once farmed them for loot, they now band together, pooling their strange abilities to build a thriving, self-sufficient industrial refuge.

---

## üéÆ Core Gameplay Loop

### 1. Survival Gathering
Start with a few weak monsters (slimes, goblins) scavenging for scraps, bones, and fungus in a ruined wilderness.

### 2. Collective Crafting
Build primitive monster "structures": slime pits, bone kilns, spider silk looms. Monsters themselves become the machines.

### 3. Emergent Industry
Monsters discover industry not out of progress, but necessity. Slimes refine metals, trolls hammer stone, ants create transport networks.

### 4. Evolution & Optimization
Evolve your workforce to create more efficient production chains. Balance different monster species for optimal resource flow.

### 5. Defense & Growth
Hero raids periodically attack, forcing balance between production chains and defenses. Use logistics monsters for defense without crippling production.

### 6. Belonging
Long-term goal: create a monstrous civilization where each species has its place, evolving into a society with its own values.

- **Acid Slimes:** Dissolve ore into raw metals
- **Hobgoblins:** Stronger haulers with larger capacity
- **Weaver Spiders:** Create silk conveyor systems

### Tier 2: Industrial Beasts

#### Heavy Workers
- **Trolls:** Crushers, refiners, heavy transport
- **Zombies:** Tireless but slow laborers
- **Bats:** Aerial couriers, bypass obstacles

#### Magical Processors
- **Wraiths:** Teleport fragile goods short distances
- **Ember Drakes:** Fire-smelters and heavy transport
- **Crystal Slimes:** Handle delicate magical materials

### Tier 3: Advanced Infrastructure

#### Living Systems
- **Ant Highways:** Bio-tunnel conveyor networks
- **Spider Silk Roads:** Permanent fast transport routes
- **Slime Processing Hubs:** Integrated refinement centers

- **Builder Ants:** Construct organic structures automatically
- **Carrier Ants:** Massive hauling capacity like living trucks
- **Royal Guard Ants:** Protect critical infrastructure

### Tier 4: Monster Civilization

#### Apex Creatures
- **Hive Mind Ants:** AI-like logistics coordination across entire map
- **Bone Colossus:** Walking refinery processing in its ribcage
- **Hydra:** Multi-headed multitasker (carry, refine, defend simultaneously)
- **Demon Forgeheart:** Living furnace for advanced materials

#### Civilization Features
- **Necropolis:** Undead workforce automation
- **Monster Megacity:** Multi-species cooperation hub
- **Bio-Machine Integration:** Creatures and structures merge into living factories

## üå≥ Monster Evolution Tech Tree

### Research System
**Science Packs** are replaced by **Knowledge Resources:**

- **Blood Knowledge (Red):** From goblin raids, enables basic research
- **Bone Totems (White):** Built from corpses, unlocks undead tier  
- **Crystal Charms (Blue):** Gathered by aerial creatures, unlocks magical logistics
- **Mutagen Essence (Green):** Distilled from evolving monsters, advanced evolutions
- **Soul Flames (Purple):** Rare late-game resource, civilization-tier unlocks

### Evolution Branches

```
                     START (Tier 0)
                     Goblins | Slimes | Wild Creatures
                          |
                     TIER 1: Basic Specialization
                     Hobgoblins | Acid Slimes | Spiders
                     + Ant Queen Milestone
                          |
                     TIER 2: Industrial Development  
                     Trolls | Undead | Elemental Creatures
                     + Ant Highway System
                          |
                     TIER 3: Infrastructure Beasts
                     Living Factories | Bio-Conveyors | Mega-Creatures
                          |
                     TIER 4: Monster Civilization
                     Hive Minds | Demon Cores | Dragon Allies
                          |
                     VICTORY: Monster Megacity
```

### Cross-Species Synergies
- **Goblin Tech + Troll Forges:** Enhanced metal production
- **Ant Highways + Spider Webs:** Redundant transport networks
- **Slime Processing + Undead Labor:** Automated refinement
- **Demon Magic + All Species:** Supernatural enhancement

## ‚öîÔ∏è External Pressure: Hero Raids

### Hero Raid System
**Heroes arrive expecting easy XP farming** but find an organized monster civilization instead.

#### Raid Mechanics
- **Periodic Attacks:** Heroes assault your territory seeking loot and XP
- **Escalating Difficulty:** Raids grow stronger as your civilization develops
- **Target Priority:** Heroes target production facilities and evolved monsters first

#### Defense Strategy
- **Living Defenses:** Monsters temporarily divert from work to defend
- **Strategic Choices:** Every defender weakens logistics temporarily
- **Evolved Defenders:** Specialized combat breeds maintain production while fighting

#### Post-Raid Benefits
- **Corpse Resources:** Defeated heroes provide bone, flesh, and equipment
- **Stolen Knowledge:** Capture enemy tactics and technologies
- **Fear Generation:** Build reputation to deter future raids

## üìà Progression & Victory Conditions

### Early Game (Survival Phase)
- **Goal:** Establish basic resource production and monster population
- **Key Unlocks:** First evolutions, basic defenses, simple automation
- **Challenges:** Resource scarcity, basic hero raids, monster management

### Mid Game (Industrial Phase)
- **Goal:** Create efficient production chains with specialized monsters
- **Key Unlocks:** Ant colonies, elemental creatures, advanced materials
- **Challenges:** Complex logistics optimization, stronger hero raids

### Late Game (Civilization Phase)
- **Goal:** Build integrated monster society with multiple species cooperation
- **Key Unlocks:** Hive minds, demon magic, dragon allies, mega-structures
- **Challenges:** Balancing diverse monster needs, epic hero raids

### Victory Conditions
1. **Monster Megacity:** Create a thriving multi-species civilization
2. **Industrial Dominance:** Out-produce nearby human settlements  
3. **Heroic Defeat:** Survive and repel the ultimate hero raid
4. **Evolutionary Apex:** Achieve maximum evolution across all monster species
